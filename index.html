Ousmane Dembélé

Ousmane Dembélé is a French professional footballer born on 15 May 1997 in Vernon, France. He plays as a forward or right winger for Paris Saint-Germain and the France national football team. Renowned for his exceptional pace, dribbling, creativity, and ability to use both feet, he is widely regarded as one of the world's top attacking players. Dembélé began his senior career at Stade Rennais F.C. before starring for Borussia Dortmund, then moving to FC Barcelona in 2017. In 2023, he joined Paris Saint-Germain, where he enjoyed a major resurgence, helping the club win domestic and European trophies. Internationally, Dembélé has represented France since 2016 and was part of the squad that won the 2018 FIFA World Cup.
